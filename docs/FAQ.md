# FAQ

## What is Muse Image?

Muse Image is Meta's agentic image generation and editing model, introduced in July 2026. Meta describes tool use through search and code, test-time self-refinement, multi-reference composition, and precise local editing. It is rolling out across selected Meta consumer surfaces, with availability varying by product and region.

Primary references:

- [Meta AI engineering overview](https://ai.meta.com/blog/introducing-muse-image-muse-video-msl/)
- [Meta product announcement](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/07/introducing-muse-image-meta-ai/)

## Is there a public Muse Image API?

The cited launch material does not announce a generally available public developer API. This repository does not infer one from consumer-product access.

## Where does the data come from?

Official cases are kept separate from community cases. Community entries are discovered through public X posts or GitHub submissions and are published only after source, author, media, model, usefulness, and duplicate review.

## Why keep source prompts in English?

Localized READMEs translate navigation, model guidance, category descriptions, titles, and summaries. Source prompt text remains traceable to the public post so readers can audit what was actually shared.
